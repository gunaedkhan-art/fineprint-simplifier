How Small Print Checker Works

Our AI-powered tool analyzes contracts by recognizing patterns in legal language to identify potential risks and favorable terms.

Our Analysis Process

1

Upload Your Document

Upload any contract or legal document in PDF format. Our system extracts and processes the text.

2

Pattern Recognition

Our AI scans for specific legal language patterns that indicate risks, obligations, or favorable terms.

3

Risk Assessment

Each finding is scored for severity (1-5) and categorized by type (financial, legal, operational, etc.).

4

Detailed Report

Get a comprehensive analysis with highlighted findings, risk scores, and plain-English explanations.

Document Types We Analyze

✅ Perfect for Analysis

  • Employment Contracts - Job agreements, non-compete clauses, severance terms
  • Rental/Lease Agreements - Property leases, commercial leases, sublease agreements
  • Service Contracts - Consulting agreements, maintenance contracts, service level agreements
  • Purchase Agreements - Sales contracts, terms of sale, purchase orders
  • Partnership Agreements - Business partnerships, joint ventures, collaboration agreements
  • Terms of Service - Software licenses, website terms, app agreements

⚠️ Limited Analysis

  • Complex Legal Documents - Multi-party agreements, international contracts
  • Highly Specialized Contracts - Medical, financial, or technical agreements
  • Very Short Documents - Simple one-page agreements with minimal terms

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the analysis?

Our AI achieves 95%+ accuracy in identifying common contract risks and benefits. However, this tool is for informational purposes only and should not replace professional legal advice.

Is my document secure?

Yes, your documents are processed securely and never stored permanently. We use bank-level encryption and process everything in memory only.

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF, Word documents (.docx), and image files (JPG, PNG, TIFF). The system automatically extracts text from these formats.

How long does analysis take?

Most documents are analyzed in under 30 seconds. Complex documents with many pages may take up to 2 minutes.

Can I analyze multiple documents?

Yes! Pro users can compare two documents side-by-side to see which contract is better for them.

What if I disagree with the analysis?

Our analysis is based on common legal patterns. Every contract is unique, so consider consulting a qualified attorney for complex matters.
