How Small Print Checker Works

Our AI-powered tool analyzes contracts by recognizing patterns in legal language to identify potential risks and favorable terms.

Our Analysis Process

1

Upload Your Document

Upload any contract or legal document in PDF format. Our system extracts and processes the text.

2

Pattern Recognition

Our AI scans for specific legal language patterns that indicate risks, obligations, or favorable terms.

3

Risk Assessment

Each finding is scored for severity (1-5) and categorized by type (financial, legal, operational, etc.).

4

Detailed Report

Get a comprehensive analysis with highlighted findings, risk scores, and plain-English explanations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of risks does the tool identify?

We identify various risk categories including financial obligations, legal liabilities, restrictive clauses, termination penalties, data privacy issues, and unfair terms.

How accurate is the analysis?

Our tool provides a comprehensive analysis based on pattern recognition, but it's designed to assist rather than replace legal advice. Always consult with a qualified attorney for complex legal matters.

Can I upload handwritten documents?

No, the tool works best with typed text. Handwritten documents or poor-quality scans may not be processed accurately.

What if my document is password-protected?

Password-protected PDFs cannot be analyzed. Please remove the password protection before uploading.

How long does the analysis take?

Most documents are analyzed within 30-60 seconds, depending on the length and complexity of the document.

Can I compare two contracts?

Yes! Pro users can upload two documents for side-by-side comparison to identify differences in terms and conditions.

Is my document data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Documents are processed securely and not stored permanently. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures.

What if the tool doesn't find any risks?

This could mean the contract is well-balanced, or it might contain risks in language patterns we haven't yet learned. We continuously improve our pattern recognition.

Can I export the analysis results?

Pro users can export analysis results in PDF, Word, or Excel formats for sharing with legal professionals or team members.

Does this replace legal advice?

No, this tool is for educational and informational purposes only. It helps you understand potential issues but should not replace consultation with a qualified attorney.
